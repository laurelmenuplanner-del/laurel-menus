## Veggie Lasagna (Veg) Yield: 3

### Ingredients:

- 1 16oz packs of lasagna noodles
- 5 cups chopped mushrooms
- 2 green bell peppers
- 2 yellow, orange, or red bell peppers
- 2 onions
- 10 cloves of garlic
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 30oz of tomato sauce
- 1.5 cups mozzarella cheese
- 2 eggs
- 1.25 lbs of ricotta
- 1/2 cups parmesan cheese
- 3 tsp dried basil
- 2 tbsp salt

### Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375F
2. Chop onions, peppers, mushrooms, and mince garlic
3. *the box will say if the noodles need to boiled or not, if so skip this step* cook for about 10 minutes or until pasta is al dente (the noodles are cooked but still firm)
4. Set the tilt skillet to medium heat and add oil.
5. After letting the tilt skillet heat up add chopped peppers, onions, and mushrooms until cooked and onions and peppers are soft.
6. Add garlic, basil, 1 tbsp salt, and pepper to the veggie mixture.
7. Then add the tomato sauce to the veggies and bring to a simmer
8. Store in a hotel pan in the walk-in fridge
   - Set aside 3 cups of this sauce for vegan lasagna!
9. Combine parmesan, ricotta, salt, pepper, and eggs in a large mixing bowl
10. Store in the walk-in fridge
11. In a SMALL (because it is a small portion) hotel pan spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of the pan, layer the noodles on top, then spread a layer of the ricotta mixture on top of noodles, and then sprinkle a layer of mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta layer.
12. Repeat this layering process until the hotel pan is almost full and sprinkle the remaining cup of parmesan cheese as the very top layer. You may need more than one hotel pan
13. Cover with foil and bake in the oven for 25 minutes then take off the foil and bake uncovered for the remaining 20-30 minutes or until cheese is melted and slightly browned on the top. Serve in a hot tray.
