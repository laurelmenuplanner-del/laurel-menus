# Teriyaki Chicken (Ca) / Teriyaki Tofu (Veg, Vn) / Stir Fry Vegetables (All) / Rice (All)

## Teriyaki Chicken (Ca) Yield: 35

**Ingredients:**

- 15 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 2 2/3 cups TAMARI GF soy sauce
  - ALTERNATIVE: Coconut Aminos
- ¾ cups honey
- ¾ cups rice vinegar
- ¾ cups brown sugar
- ¾ cups sesame seeds
- 11 cloves minced garlic
- ¾ cups minced ginger

**Instructions:**

1. Prepare the marinade first.
2. Place the chicken into two hotel pans and then pour the marinade over them. Mix it and try to get all the chicken coated. Set aside to marinate.
3. Preheat the oven to 425F.
4. Bake chicken in a preheated oven for 20 minutes.
5. Flip the thighs over every 10 minutes now. Begin checking internal temp once 30 minutes have passed. Temp should reach 165F around 40 minutes. Pull the chicken out once the largest chicken thigh reads 165F.

## Teriyaki Tofu Yield: 6

**Ingredients:**

- 3 packages firm tofu

**Baking mix:**

- 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
- 1.5 tbsp cooking oil
- 1.5 tbsp cornstarch
- ¼ tsp white pepper

**Teriyaki Sauce:**

- ⅛ cup water
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ⅗ tbsp maple syrup
- ⅗ tbsp rice vinegar
- ⅗ tbsp sesame oil
- ⅗ tbsp brown sugar
- ⅗ tbsp sesame seeds
- 1.5 cloves minced garlic
- ⅗ tbsp minced ginger

**Thickening agent:**

- 1.5 tbsp chilled water
- 0.75 tbsp cornstarch

**Instructions:**

1. To make the crispy baked tofu, preheat the oven to 400° and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Place the tofu into a medium bowl and add the soy sauce, oil, cornstarch, and pepper. Toss to coat the tofu.
3. Arrange the tofu pieces in a single layer on the baking sheet.
4. Bake for about 30 minutes, turning halfway through, until browned and crispy.
5. While the tofu bakes, mix together the teriyaki sauce ingredients in a pot.
6. Place the sauce on medium heat and bring to a simmer.
7. Lower the heat and allow to simmer for 10 minutes.
8. Stir the chilled water and cornstarch together in a small bowl or cup. Stir the mixture into the sauce and allow it to simmer for about 1 minute more, until it thickens up a bit.
9. Remove the pot from the heat.
10. When the tofu is finished cooking, add it to the pot and stir to coat the tofu with sauce.
    - Wait until just ready to serve to mix the sauce and the tofu.

## Stir Fry Vegetables (All) Yield: 40

**Ingredients:**

- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 10 bell peppers, sliced
- 1.5 lbs sugar snap peas (Frozen HEB)
- 12 carrots, sliced
  - GUFF THE REST (MOVE TO REACH IN)
- 48 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 1.5 lbs broccoli
- 0.5 lbs net wt. water chestnuts (canned)
- 1.25 cup soy sauce
- 16 garlic cloves, minced
- ⅘ cup brown sugar
- 1/8 cup sesame oil
- 4 2/5 cups vegetable broth
- 1/4 cup cornstarch

**Instructions:**

1. In a tilt skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper, peas, carrots, mushrooms, broccoli, and water chestnuts. Sauté 2-3 minutes until the veggies are almost tender.
2. In a bowl whisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, vegetable broth, and cornstarch.
3. Pour over veggies and cook until the sauce has thickened. Serve in hot tray.
