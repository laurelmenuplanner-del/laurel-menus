# Sun-Dried Tomato Wraps

## Ingredients

### Tortillas

- 40 large flour tortillas

### Sun-dried tomato spread / filling base

- 6 cups chopped sun-dried tomatoes, oil-packed and drained
- 5-6 cups cream cheese
- 3 cups grated Parmesan
- 4 cups fresh spinach leaves
- 4 tbsp minced garlic
- Salt & pepper, to taste

### Sun-dried tomato spread / filling base

- 60 slices of chicken breast turkey
- 40 slices of Provolone cheese slices
- 8 cups chopped lettuce
- 4-5 large cucumbers
- 3 large red bell peppers
- 2 cups olive (sliced), optional

## Instructions

1. **Prep the spread/base**
   - Drain and chop the sun-dried tomatoes
   - In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cream cheese, Parmesan, garlic, salt and pepper. Mix until smooth and creamy.
2. **Lay out the Tortillas**
   - Place all 40 tortillas flat on a clean surface or sheet trays for assembly. Or you can just do this in batches (prolly the best)
3. **Assemble the Wraps**
   - Spread about ¼ cup of the tomato mixture on each tortilla, leaving a ½ inch border
   - Layer with about 1 slice of cheese, then 2 slices of meat
     - NOTE: FOR AT LEAST 8 OF THESE, DO NOT PUT MEAT. This will be the vegetarian option. Make sure you know which 8 they are and put them out separately :)
   - Add about ¼ cup spinach and a small handful of (¼ cup) of shredded lettuce
   - Add about a few strips of cucumber or red pepper and olives if desired
4. **Roll Tightly:**
   - Fold in the edges slightly and roll each wrap tightly
5. **Cut & Serve**
   - Slice each wrap in half
   - Arrange on platters, cover, and refrigerate until serving

# Caesar Salad (all) Yield: 65

## Ingredients:

### Caesar Salad dressing

- 18 small garlic cloves, minced
- ¼ cup +4 Tbsp dijon mustard
- 1/4 cup Annie's Organic Worcestershire Sauce (certified gluten free)
- ¼ cup +3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- 4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 3 Tbsp sea salt, or to taste
- 3 Tbsp black pepper, plus more to serve

### Cesar Salad

- 4 large romaine lettuce
- 7 cup parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved (kept on the side please for vegans)

## Directions

### For the dressing:

1. In a small bowl, whisk together garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.
2. Slowly drizzle in extra virgin olive oil while whisking constantly.
3. Whisk in salt and black pepper, or season to taste.

### For the salad:

1. Rinse, dry and chop or tear the romaine into bite-sized pieces. Place in a large serving bowl
2. Top with dressing and toss gently until lettuce is evenly coated.
3. Serve in cold table with the parmesan cheese on the side
