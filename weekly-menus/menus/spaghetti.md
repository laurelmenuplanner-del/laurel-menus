# Spaghetti w/Red Sauce

**Yield: 42**

## Spaghetti Ingredients:

- 6 16 oz packs of Spaghetti Noodles
- ½ Pound gluten free pasta
- ¼ cup + 2 Tbsp Salt
- 6 red bell peppers
- 6 orange bell peppers
- 3 onions
- 200 oz Canned crushed tomatoes
- 30 cloves of garlic
- 6 cups of mushrooms (chopped)
- 1.25 cups olive oil
- 1.25 cups vegan butter
- 1 Tbsp + 2 tsp black pepper
- 2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 tsp dried oregano

## Spinach Salad Ingredients:

**Yield: 50 servings**

- 1 large bag of spinach
- 8 carts of cherry tomatoes
- 3 red onions
- 4 cups crumbled feta

## Salad Dressing Ingredients:

- 5 cups of olive oil
- 2.5 cups of lemon juice
- 10 cloves of garlic
- 2 heaping Tbsp of dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

---

## Directions

### Spaghetti

1. Bring a large pot of water to boiling and add in ¼ cup of salt to the boiling water. Mix salt so that it is incorporated into the water.
2. Once the water is boiling, put the spaghetti noodles in.
3. Boil gluten free spaghetti in a separate pot
4. Cook spaghetti noodles until they are al dente. They are fully cooked but firm.
5. Drain spaghetti and transfer to a hotel pan. Use a few tablespoons of olive oil to coat the spaghetti so that the noodles don't stick. Use tongs to mix around the noodles and olive oil.
6. Serve the noodles in the hot tray
7. To cook the sauce: Chop the red bell pepper, orange bell pepper, onions, and mince garlic. *The mushrooms should be pre-chopped. If they are not, then you will need to chop them.*
8. Turn on the tilt skillet to medium heat and add 2 cups of oil.
9. Let the oil heat up for a few minutes.
10. Once the oil is hot, add in the chopped bell peppers and onions. Cook these for about 5 minutes.
11. Next, add in the mushrooms, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes. Cook the veggies until the bell peppers are soft and the onions are translucent.
12. Add in the canned crushed/pureed tomatoes to the tilt skillet. Mix so that the veggies are well combined with the tomato sauce.
13. Next, add in the 2 cups of butter, 2 Tbsp of salt, dried oregano, and black pepper. Mix ingredients into the sauce for about 10 minutes and bring sauce to a simmer.
14. Let the sauce simmer for another 10 minutes, while mixing periodically so it doesn't burn on the bottom.
15. Mix some of the sauce with the gluten free pasta and serve seperately
16. Once the sauce is done, mix with pasta, transfer to a hotel pan and serve in hot tray.

### Salad:

1. Chop red onions
2. Wash cherry tomatoes
3. Add spinach, onions, crumbled feta and cherry tomatoes to a large bowl and mix together
4. Serve in cold tray

### Salad Dressing:

1. Combine lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, dried oregano, salt and pepper to blender and combine until smooth
2. Serve in cold tray next to salad
