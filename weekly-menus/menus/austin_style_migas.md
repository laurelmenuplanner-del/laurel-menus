# Austin Style Migas

## Austin Style Migas Yield: 35

**Ingredients:**

- 25 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 6.25 cup red onion, diced
- 50 roma tomatoes, cored and diced
- 10 jalapeños, seeded and diced
- 25 garlic clove, finely chopped
- 125 eggs, beaten
- 6.25 cup tortilla chips, crumbled
- 6.25 cup jack cheese, shredded
- 2 bunches cilantro, chopped
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 60 tortillas
- 5 limes, quartered
- 30 oz salsa

**Directions:**

1. Heat the oil in the tilt skillet over medium heat. Add the onion, tomato, jalapeño and garlic, as well as some salt and pepper. Cook for 6 minutes, stirring until softened.
2. Add the eggs, stir and let them cook for a little, about 3 minutes. Then add the crumbled chips. Stir in the cheese then remove from the skillet.
3. Sprinkle with cilantro, season to taste with more salt and pepper and serve in a hotel pan, alongside the tortillas, limes and salsa.
