# Jaeger Schnitzel / French Fries / Salad / Drinks

## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 8 cups of bread crumbs
- 8 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 18 lbs of boneless, skinless thighs, pounded thin
- 1 cups of vegetable oil
- 8 medium onions
- 120 oz sliced mushrooms
- 12.5 cups water
- 8 cups beef bouillon
- 4 cups sour cream
- 5 tbsp cornstarch
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- ¾ cup flour

## Instructions

1. **Prep Coating**
   - In one large bowl or pan, beat the eggs
   - In another large tray, mix 8 cups breadcrumbs, ¾ cup flour, salt, and pepper
2. **Prepare Chicken:**
   - This is gonna be the bulk of the work
   - Wrap the chicken in saram wrap and then pound it into cutlets with a hammer (should be in the kitchen near walk in)
   - Dip each in beaten egg, then dredge in breadcrumb mixture to coat well
     - FOR 2 CHICKEN THIGHS, DO NOT DO THIS. COOK THESE NORMALLY. THIS IS FOR OUR DF/GF FRIEND. COOK IN SEPARATE PAN
3. **Pan-Fry Chicken:**
   - Heat vegetable oil in tilt skillet over medium-high heat
   - Fry cutlets until golden brown and cooked through (about 4-5 minutes – make sure its fully cooked at 165F internally)
   - Keep warm on sheet pans in a low oven (200F) – ask if you're not sure what this means, but essentially
4. **Make the garlic-onion sauce**
   - TEMPORARY FIX:
     - Ingredients:
       1. 12 garlic cloves
       2. 8 onions
       3. 4 tsp thyme leaves
       4. 8 cups of hot water and 8 bouillon cubes.
       5. 14 tbsp butter
       6. 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
     - NOTE: It should be thick. So throw more butter if it's watery. Or just @ me in discord again
     - Go to step 6. Ignore the sour cream and mushroom
5. **Make the mushroom sauce**
   - In the tilt skillet, saute onions and mushrooms in some of the remaining oil until soft and browned
   - Add 12.5 cups of water and 8 bouillon cubes. Stir and simmer gently for 15 minutes
6. **Finish the sauce:**
   - In a bowl, whisk together 4 cups of sour cream and 8 tbsp cornstarch until smooth
   - Gradually stir this into the hot mushroom mixture over low heat, stirring constantly until it thickens (not boiled)
7. **Serve:**

# Eggplant Shnitzel

## Ingredients

- 8 eggplants
- 210 g all-purpose flour
- 4 eggs, medium
- 210 g panko breadcrumbs, or dried breadcrumbs
- sunflower oil, for the frying (can just be olive oil)
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Leave the skin on the eggplants and use a fork to prick them over the whole surface.
2. Bake them in a preheated oven at 350°F set to fan for 30 minutes. Increase the temperature to 450°F, and bake them for another 10 minutes until their skin is burnt, making sure to turn them over continuously.
   - Note: the timing for this might be incorrect. You have to continuously check yourself.
3. Transfer the eggplants to a baking pan, immediately cover the pan with plastic wrap, and let the eggplants cool for 20-30 minutes.
4. Remove the plastic wrap and peel the eggplants with your hands, keeping the stem on.
5. Transfer the eggplants to a cutting board, and press them down with a spoon to spread them.
6. Season them with salt and pepper on both sides.
7. Put the flour into a baking pan, the eggs into a bowl, whisking them with a fork, and the panko breadcrumbs into another bowl.
8. Place a frying pan over high heat, add the sunflower oil, and let it heat up well.
9. Spread some paper towels in a baking pan, and set it aside.
10. Dredge one eggplant at a time in the flour, then dip it into the eggs and the panko, and add it to the pan.
11. Cook each side for 1-2 minutes, use a slotted spoon to transfer the eggplant schnitzel to the baking pan, and press it with the paper towels to absorb the excess oil.
12. Follow the same process for the remaining eggplants, and remove the pan from the heat.

# Caesar Salad (all) Yield: 65

## Ingredients:

### Caesar Salad dressing

- 11 small garlic cloves, minced
- 0.25 cups dijon mustard
- 0.175 cups Annie's Organic Worcestershire Sauce (certified gluten free)
- 0.175 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 0.175 cups red wine vinegar
- 2.5 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1.5 Tbsp sea salt, or to taste
- 1.5 Tbsp black pepper, plus more to serve

### Cesar Salad

- 4 large romaine lettuce
- 3.5 cup parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved (kept on the side please for vegans)

## Directions

### For the dressing:

1. In a small bowl, whisk together garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.
2. Slowly drizzle in extra virgin olive oil while whisking constantly.
3. Whisk in salt and black pepper, or season to taste.

### For the salad:

1. Rinse, dry and chop or tear the romaine into bite-sized pieces. Place in a large serving bowl
2. Top with dressing and toss gently until lettuce is evenly coated.
3. Serve in cold table with the parmesan cheese on the side
